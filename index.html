About
MALAVA CATHOLIC
Parish

MALAVA CATHOLIC Parish is a vibrant faith community serving the people of Malava, dedicated to spreading the Gospel and providing spiritual and social support to all parishioners. We offer numerous ministries, including youth programs, bible study, and community outreach, to bring the community closer together in faith.

Our mission is to guide our parishioners in their spiritual journey, supporting them through worship, community, and service. We are committed to promoting peace, love, and understanding among all members of our congregation.

Rev.Fr Vincent Sanga

Dean & Parish Priest

"Guiding our parish with love and devotion."
